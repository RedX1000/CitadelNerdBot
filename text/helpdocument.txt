Commands 
======== 
$help = Brings this message up 
$hello = Says hello 
$inspire = Gives an inspirational quote 
$refresh = Refresh clan member list 
$clanlist <order> = Displays full list of members 
	- "id" or leave blank to sort by ID
	- "alpha" for alphabetical order
	- "rank" to sort by rank
	- "citadel" to check who capped and visited
